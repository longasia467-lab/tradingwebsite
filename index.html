Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the course duration?
The course is self-paced with lifetime access. Most students complete the core video modules within 1 week (approx 12-15 hours of content). However, mastering the setup usually takes about 2-3 weeks of live market observation. You can watch the recordings as many times as you like.
What is the refund policy?
Honestly? You won't want one. Once you see the power of the Gauntlet Model and start executing trades with precision, you'll be too busy compounding your profits to think about a refund. However, strictly speaking, due to immediate access to our proprietary IP, all sales are final.
Are there custom indicators included?
Yes! The Emerald Plan includes 2 core indicators for trend identification. The Ruby and Diamond Plans include our advanced "Institutional Tracker" suite (5 indicators total) which helps spot hidden order blocks and liquidity grabs automatically on TradingView.
I am a complete beginner. Is this for me?
Absolutely. We start from the very basics (Module 1: Market Structure & Candles) before moving to advanced concepts. You do not need any prior finance knowledge.
Does the Algo Bot place trades automatically?
Yes, for Diamond members, the Algo Bot can be linked to supported brokers for fully automated execution. For Emerald and Ruby members, the bot sends high-accuracy signals to your phone/Telegram, which you can execute manually with one click.
